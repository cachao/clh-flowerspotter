Climate Calendar / Calendr Hinsawdd

The wildflowers planted in this dry stone wall are all part of the local flower palate that changes through the seasons. They are planted to bloom through the year in order from north to south (or left to right as you face the wooden fence) during the months indicated: a calendar of flowers. As climate change brings warmer springs and shorter winters, the blooms may appear outside their start month, or open out of order. Dry stone wall built by Aeryn Jones.

Mae’r blodau gwyllt sydd wedi’u plannu yn y wal gerrig hon i gyd yn rhan o’r darlun blodau lleol sy’n newid gyda’r tymhorau. Maen nhw wedi’u plannu i flodeuo trwy gydol y flwyddyn mewn trefn o’r gogledd i’r de (neu o’r chwith i’r dde wrth i chi wynebu’r ffens pren) yn ystod y misoedd a ddangosir: sef calendr o flodau. Wrth i newid yn yr hinsawdd ddod â gwanwynau cynhesach a gaeafau byrrach, gall y blodau ymddangos y tu allan i’w mis dechreuol, neu agor mewn gwahanol drefn. Aeryn Jones adeiladodd y wall gerrig.
